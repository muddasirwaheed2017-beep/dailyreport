## SECTION 9: HOW CHATGPT SHOULD WRITE META TITLES

### Formula:
```
[Product Name] [Key Spec] | [Price] | CNC Electric Pakistan
```

### Rules:
1. **Length:** 50-60 characters (never over 60)
2. **Must include:** "CNC Electric" or "CNC Electric Pakistan"
3. **Must include:** Primary keyword from Section 2 (if the product matches)
4. **Should include:** Price (e.g., "Rs. 425") or price range
5. **Should include:** "Pakistan" if space allows
6. **Separator:** Use `|` between segments
7. **No stuffing:** Max 2 keywords, natural reading

### Examples (Good):
```
1 Pole MCB 2A-63A | Rs. 425 | CNC Electric Pakistan
4 Pole ATS 63A-250A | Auto Transfer Switch | CNC Electric
DC SPD 1000V Solar Surge Protector | CNC Electric Pakistan
Fire Extinguisher 2kg-9kg | Shop CNC Electric Pakistan
```

### Examples (Bad — avoid these patterns):
```
❌ "1 Pole MCB" (too short, no brand, no price)
❌ "Buy Best Quality 1 Pole MCB AC Breaker Circuit Breaker Pakistan CNC" (stuffed)
❌ "CNC Electric — Your Trusted Partner for Electrical Solutions" (generic, no product)
```

## SECTION 10: HOW CHATGPT SHOULD WRITE META DESCRIPTIONS

### Formula:
```
[CTA] [product] [key spec] [benefit]. [Trust signal]. [Price/delivery info].
```

### Rules:
1. **Length:** 120-155 characters (sweet spot is 140-150)
2. **Must start with CTA:** "Buy", "Shop", "Order", "Get"
3. **Must include:** Primary keyword naturally
4. **Must include:** "Pakistan" somewhere
5. **Must include:** One trust signal: "5-year warranty", "IEC certified", "COD available", "free delivery"
6. **Should include:** Price or "starting from Rs. X"
7. **End with period.** Never end mid-sentence.

### Examples (Good):
```
Buy CNC 1 Pole MCB breaker 2A-63A in Pakistan. IEC 60898 certified, 5-year warranty. Starting Rs. 425. Free delivery, COD available.
(134 chars)

Shop automatic transfer switch (ATS) 63A-250A for generator backup. CNC Electric Pakistan. Free delivery + 5-year warranty.
(124 chars)

Order DC circuit breakers for solar panels — 1000V/1500V rated. CNC Electric Pakistan. From Rs. 650. COD + free shipping.
(121 chars)
```

### Examples (Bad):
```
❌ "We offer a wide range of electrical products including MCBs and circuit breakers for homes and businesses across Pakistan" (no CTA, no brand, no price)
❌ "CNC Electric" (way too short)
❌ "Buy the best quality premium grade industrial MCB miniature circuit breaker AC breaker safety breaker..." (keyword stuffed, truncated)
```
