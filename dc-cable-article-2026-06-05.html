1. What is solar DC cable?

Solar DC cable is double-insulated copper wire designed for direct-current PV-string applications. It must handle:

  • High UV exposure (panels are outside under Pakistani sun)
  • Wide temperature range -10C to +120C in cable jacket
  • 1000-1500V DC voltage rating
  • Continuous current at maximum panel Isc

Standard certifications: IEC 62930, TUV, IEC 60332-1 fire rating.

3. When to pick which

Pick 6mm²

  • Residential PV strings under 25A Isc continuous (most 5-8 kW residential)
  • Cable runs under 30 metres between panels and combiner box
  • Voltage drop is acceptable (under 2%)
  • Budget-conscious install

Pick 10mm²

  • String currents 25-40A continuous (larger 10+ kW residential or commercial)
  • Cable runs over 30 metres (voltage drop minimisation matters)
  • Heavy ground-mount installs with long DC cable home-runs
  • Industrial PV applications

5. Sizing for your install

Step-by-step:

  1. Look up panel Isc on the datasheet (e.g. Jinko 580W Isc = ~13.5A).
  2. Calculate string Isc: panel Isc × number of parallel strings. For 2-string parallel: 13.5 × 2 = 27A.
  3. Add 25% safety margin: 27 × 1.25 = 33.75A.
  4. Pick cable: 6mm² rated 32A is borderline; choose 10mm² rated 45A for headroom.
  5. Calculate voltage drop for your cable length:
    VD = (Length × 2 × current × resistance per metre)
    For 30m of 10mm² at 27A: 30 × 2 × 27 × 0.00183 = 2.96V (1.2% at 250V — acceptable).

6. Installation

  1. Run red cable for positive, black cable for negative. Label both with shrink-wrap markers.
  2. Use rubber grommets where cable enters combiner box or conduit.
  3. Avoid sharp bends (minimum bend radius = 6× cable diameter).
  4. Use UV-rated cable ties or stainless clamps to fix to mounting structure.
  5. Terminate with MC4 connectors at panel side, ring terminals or MC4 at inverter side.
  6. Bond shield (if shielded cable) to earth at the combiner box only.

7. FAQ

Cheapest DC cable in Pakistan 2026?

CNC 4mm² PV at PKR 85/m for small caravan or single-panel runs. For typical residential: CNC 6mm² at PKR 130/m.

Will 4mm² work for a 5 kW PV string?

Depends on string Isc and length. 4mm² is rated 24A in Pakistani 47°C ambient. For 5 kW system with 10A Isc string under 20m, yes. Above 25A or 20m: step up to 6mm².

Do I need TUV-certified cable or is generic OK?

NEPRA 2026 doesn't explicitly mandate TUV. PEC code accepts IEC 62930 compliant. Generic Chinese DC cable usually claims IEC compliance; verify on the cable jacket print. TUV adds ~40% to cost for marginal benefit in Pakistani installations.

Why are DC cables sold in red/black pairs?

Convention: red = positive, black = negative. Many manufacturers sell pre-paired reels to simplify ordering. You can also buy single-colour reels and use heat-shrink labels.
