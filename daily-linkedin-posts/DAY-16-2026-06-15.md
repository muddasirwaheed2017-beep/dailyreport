## Smart Curtain Switch & Motorised Curtain Motor

---

**Post:**

"Just buy a curtain motor" — three words that cost Pakistani homeowners between Rs. 3,500 and Rs. 12,000 when the system doesn't work.

Here's what actually goes wrong and how to avoid it:

**The compatibility triangle nobody talks about:**
Motor → Switch → Mechanical coupling. Mismatch any one layer and the system fails. A track rail motor is not interchangeable with a rod-curtain motor. A Wi-Fi switch that expects neutral will not work in a 2-wire Pakistani switch box.

**The no-neutral problem in Pakistan:**
Most apartments built before 2015 only have live + load at the switch position — no neutral. A standard Wi-Fi curtain switch needs neutral. The solution: no-neutral compatible switches (Rs. 1,800–2,800) that draw a small bypass current through the motor coil. Works — but requires the motor to tolerate that bypass current. Read the spec sheet before ordering.

**The polarity swap problem:**
Pakistani DB boxes frequently have live and neutral reversed at the switch feed. A no-neutral switch assumes a specific wire is live. Reversed polarity = switch won't power up. Always verify with a phase tester before installation.

**2026 PKR price matrix:**
• RF 433 MHz: Rs. 850–1,400 (no internet dependency — best for load-shedding areas)
• Wi-Fi no-neutral: Rs. 1,800–2,800 (app + voice control with existing 2-wire wiring)
• Wi-Fi + RF hybrid: Rs. 2,200–3,500 (app with physical remote backup)
• Built-in motor curtain rail (per metre): Rs. 2,500–5,000
• DIN-rail timer controller: Rs. 3,200–5,500 (office/industrial)
