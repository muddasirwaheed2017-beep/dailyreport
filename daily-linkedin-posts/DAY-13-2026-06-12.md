**The LFP battery payback calculation Pakistani solar installers need to do — but almost never do.**

Here is the math for a 48V 100Ah system in Pakistan, June 2026:

**Option 1: SLA (Sealed Lead Acid)**
- Cost: Rs. 1,10,000
- Cycle life: 300 cycles (80% DoD)
- 5-year replacement count: 3–4 replacements
- 5-year total: Rs. 4,40,000+

**Option 2: LFP (LiFePO4)**
- Cost: Rs. 1,95,000
- Cycle life: 3,500 cycles (80% DoD)
- 5-year replacement count: 0
- 5-year total: Rs. 1,95,000

**Payback on the premium: ~18–24 months.**

The variable that kills LFP ROI: unprotected installation. A single ground fault without a DC circuit breaker can destroy a Rs. 2 lakh battery in milliseconds.

IEC 62619:2022 §6.3 requires protection components (fuse/DCDB/BMS disconnect) for every lithium battery system connected to an inverter.
