NEPRA 2026 solar tubewell rule changes

Pakistani solar-powered tubewell installations face new 2026 NEPRA rules:

  • Solar pump systems above 5 HP require DC isolator (IEC 60947-3) + DC fuse (IEC 60269-6 gPV) on PV string side
  • Solar pump controller (VFD-style) must have IEC 61800-2 certification
  • For net-metered tubewell installations with grid backup: hybrid inverter (UL 1741) required
  • Dry-running protection mandatory on solar pump systems (water-level sensor + cut-off)
  • Earth bond on motor housing + PV frame + tubewell casing per IEC 60364 (Pakistani inspector check)

Six mistakes Pakistani tubewell motor buyers make

  1. Buying motor only, skipping protection bundle — motor fails in 12-24 months without protection
  2. Using AC motor for solar without VFD — can't run on variable solar power
  3. Wrong cable size — voltage drop causes motor to burn out
  4. Skipping dry-running protection on solar pump — motor cooks itself in 1-2 hours of dry running
  5. No phase failure relay on 3-phase motor — single-phasing destroys motor in minutes
  6. Wrong starter type for HP — DOL starter on 15+ HP motor causes grid voltage drops + breaker trips
