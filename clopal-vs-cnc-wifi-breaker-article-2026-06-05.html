Where Clopal wins

Price. Rs 4,105 vs CNC's Rs 7,800-9,200 is a 47-55% cost difference. For a home automation enthusiast wiring 6-8 circuits with budget under PKR 50,000, Clopal scales further. CNC at Rs 9,200 per circuit means PKR 73,600 for 8 circuits vs Clopal at PKR 32,840.

Adjustable thresholds. The Clopal 63A model lets you set over-voltage anywhere from 110V to 300V, under-voltage 85V to 220V, leakage 10-300 mA, current 1-63A. CNC YCB10-W has fixed per-SKU current rating (16A / 20A / 32A separate models) and fixed leakage at 30 mA. Clopal's adjustability is genuinely useful for Pakistani voltage swings where you may want to set under-voltage to 170V instead of the typical 180V default.

Smart-home ecosystem breadth. Clopal also makes WiFi gas detector, door/window sensor, motion sensor, curtain opener, IR universal remote, and 10-inch Tuya control panel. If you want to build a full Clopal smart home using a single brand, the breakers integrate cleanly with their sensors.

Single-app simplicity. Both Clopal SKUs work in Smart Life and Tuya app without additional configuration. CNC YCB10-W works in Tuya and eWeLink — broader compatibility but more decisions for non-technical buyers.

Where CNC YCB10-W wins

IEC certification. CNC YCB10-W is IEC 60898-1 + 60947-2 certified — these are the international circuit-breaker standards that Pakistani Electric Inspectors and PEC look for during commercial / industrial installations. Clopal's WiFi smart breakers don't publicly list IEC certifications. For DB-box installations that need inspection sign-off, certification matters.

Breaking capacity (kA). CNC YCB10-W has 6 kA breaking at 230V AC — meaning it can safely interrupt a 6 kA short circuit. Clopal's WiFi breakers don't publicly specify breaking capacity. In a real short-circuit event (when the AC compressor shorts to chassis at 3 AM), the breaker either interrupts safely or fails catastrophically. CNC's 6 kA spec gives confidence here.

Trip curve selectivity. CNC offers B curve (resistive — LED lighting), C curve (most residential — AC, fridge, motor), D curve (inductive — large motors, transformers). Clopal's WiFi relay-based design doesn't offer selectable trip curves — they're a different category of device (smart relay, not smart MCB in the strict IEC sense).

5-year warranty. CNC distributors in Pakistan honour a 5-year warranty on YCB10-W series. Clopal's typical Pakistani warranty is 1 year. Over a 5-year period, the warranty difference matters financially when a device dies in year 3.

Industrial-grade build. CNC YCB10-W is built to industrial-installation standards — designed for 24/7 operation at full rated current. Clopal WiFi breakers are positioned for residential / light commercial use. Different target customer.

Home Assistant native integration. CNC YCB10-W via Tuya local API integrates cleanly with Home Assistant for local-control automation (no cloud dependency). Clopal works through Smart Life only — cloud dependency means a 1-3 second lag and no automation when internet is down.

Use case decision tree

Pick Clopal WiFi Smart Energy Meter (Rs 4,105) if:

  • You're a home-automation enthusiast wanting monitoring without deep electrical knowledge
  • Budget is tight and you want to start with 1-2 smart circuits
  • You don't need IEC certification or 5-yr warranty
  • You're already in the Smart Life / Tuya ecosystem

Pick Clopal WiFi 63A Earth Leakage (Rs 6,420) if:

  • You want all-in-one leakage + voltage + monitoring on a single circuit
  • Your Pakistani area has wild voltage swings (170-280V) requiring custom-set under/over voltage cuts
  • You're protecting a single high-value appliance (3-ton AC, 100L geyser, sensitive electronics)
  • You don't need PEC inspection sign-off

Pick CNC YCB10-W (Rs 7,800-9,200) if:

  • Commercial or industrial install requiring PEC inspection
  • You need IEC 60898-1 + 60947-2 certified equipment for solar prosumer or NEPRA compliance
  • Multi-circuit DB box (8+ circuits) where 5-yr warranty cost-of-ownership matters
  • You want Home Assistant native local-control without Tuya cloud dependency
  • Your loads include large motors needing D-curve trip selectivity

Pick a regular non-WiFi MCB + dedicated voltage protector if:

  • You never plan to remotely control the circuit
  • Budget is < PKR 5,000 per circuit for full protection
  • You don't have reliable WiFi at the DB box location

FAQ

Q: Is Clopal a Pakistani brand or imported?
A: Clopal is a Pakistani-owned brand. Manufacturing is China-sourced (like most consumer electrical brands worldwide) but the brand, distribution, and warranty are Pakistani.

Q: Can I mix Clopal WiFi switches with CNC WiFi MCB in one home?
A: Yes. Both work with Smart Life / Tuya app. CNC YCB10-W sits at DB-box level (whole-circuit control). Clopal WiFi switches sit at wall level (single-load control). They complement each other — they don't compete on the same install point.

Q: Which is better warranty-wise — Clopal or CNC?
A: CNC offers a 5-year warranty on YCB10-W series via authorized distributors. Clopal's typical Pakistani warranty is 1 year. Over a 5-year period this matters financially.

Q: Does either satisfy NEPRA 2026 prosumer Type B RCBO mandate?
A: Neither standard WiFi MCB satisfies the Type B RCBO mandate for solar inverter feeds. Use a dedicated Type B RCBO on that specific feed.

Q: Will Clopal WiFi breaker work with Home Assistant?
A: Yes via Tuya cloud integration, but it requires internet connection for local control (cloud-dependent latency). CNC YCB10-W can be paired locally via Tuya local API without cloud.

Q: What's the breaking capacity difference and why does it matter?
A: CNC YCB10-W specifies 6 kA breaking at 230V — meaning it can safely interrupt a 6,000-amp short-circuit fault. Clopal's WiFi breakers don't publicly specify breaking capacity. In a worst-case short-circuit event, the safer-spec'd device wins. For industrial / commercial installs, breaking capacity is a hard requirement.

Q: Are Clopal WiFi breakers safe for protecting a 1.5-ton AC?
A: Yes for residential use — the 1-63A current range and 110-300V over-voltage adjustment cover AC operation. For commercial installs requiring IEC certification + PEC sign-off, CNC YCB10-W is the safer choice.
