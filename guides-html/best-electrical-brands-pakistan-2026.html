5. Chint Pakistan — Budget Chinese Brand

Chint (China) is the world's largest MCB manufacturer by unit volume. Their NB1 MCB series has flooded Pakistan's market with very competitive pricing, making Chint popular with budget-conscious contractors.

Critical Warning About Chint in Pakistan

⚠️ Counterfeit Chint products are widespread in Pakistan's open market. Sub-standard "Chint" MCBs that fail IEC 60898-1 interrupt rating tests have been documented in multiple Pakistani cities. Always verify authenticity via Chint's official Pakistan distributor and demand test certificates.

Chint Pakistan Pricing 2026

  • NB1 MCB 1P 6–32A: Rs 220–380/pole (authentic)
  • NXBLE RCBO 1P+N: Rs 1,800–2,800
  • No WiFi smart range available in Pakistan
  • 1-year warranty only

Best for: High-volume contractor projects where absolute lowest unit cost is the priority and product life cycle is short. Not recommended when authentic sourcing cannot be guaranteed — in which case CNC Electric MCBs offer better authenticity assurance at similar price points with a 5-year warranty.
