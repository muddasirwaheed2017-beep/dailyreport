### Post 2 — Sizing Mistake Hook
```
Your changeover switch is probably undersized.

The formula Pakistani electricians skip:

Total watts ÷ 220V = amps
Amps × 1.25 = changeover size

Example: 3-bedroom house, 3,200W load
3200 ÷ 220 = 14.5A
14.5 × 1.25 = 18.1A → Buy 32A minimum

Most people buy the cheap 16A and wonder
why it burns within 18 months.
```

```
⚠️ The most dangerous changeover wiring error in Pakistan:

Connecting generator neutral to WAPDA neutral
WITHOUT a break-before-make switch.

Result:
❌ Back-feed current on WAPDA neutral
❌ Electrocution risk for linemen
❌ Potential fire at your DB board

The fix: Any IEC 60947-6-1 ATS has
```
