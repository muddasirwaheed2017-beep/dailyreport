**Tweet 3 (Protocol Comparison)**

Protocol matters for Pakistan specifically:

WiFi-only: max 25–30 devices. Fails without internet.
Zigbee 3.0: 50–250+ devices. Self-healing mesh. Works OFFLINE.
Z-Wave 800: Best interference immunity. Devices hard to source locally.
Matter/Thread: Future-proof. Limited devices in PK market right now.

For most Pakistani homes → Zigbee 3.0 wins.
