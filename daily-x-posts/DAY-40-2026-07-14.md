## Tweet 2 (Pakistan UV Cracking Problem)

Why does Pakistani PVC conduit crack in 3–5 years?

Standard PVC: rated 60°C max
South wall in Karachi summer: 65–75°C surface temp

→ Exceeds rating every summer
→ UV radiation adds polymer degradation
→ Conduit cracks, cable exposed

Fix: Heavy-duty UV-resistant PVC (grey) or GI.

Standard white PVC = false economy on any Pakistan rooftop.

## Tweet 4 (Motor Flexible Conduit)

Most common Pakistan industrial conduit failure:

Rigid conduit to motor terminal box → fractures in 12–24 months.

Motor vibrates → conduit is fixed to wall → micro-movement at joint → fatigue crack.

Fix: 1.0–1.5m flexible conduit section at EVERY motor terminal box.

Rs. 90–110/m (standard) | Rs. 150–180/m (liquid-tight for outdoor pumps).

A 1.5m length costs ~Rs. 165–270. Saves months of maintenance downtime.

## Tweet 5 (Standalone — Fill Ratio)

Pakistan electricians: conduit overfilling is a fire hazard in our summers.

IEC standard: 40% fill ratio max
Pakistan reality: 45°C ambient already de-rates cable capacity
Pakistan rule: 35% fill max

25mm conduit (ID ~21mm, area 346mm²):
→ Max cables: 121mm² combined area (35% rule)

Overfill → cables overheat → insulation degradation → fault.

Size up the conduit. It costs Rs. 30/m more. The fault costs Rs. 30,000.
