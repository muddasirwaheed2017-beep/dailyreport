## Thread Post 2 (reply)

Pakistan factory floor in 45°C summer?

Pilot lamps failing every few months?

4 root causes:
1. Voltage mismatch (220VAC lamp on 24VDC) → burns in 1 second
2. Summer heat → LED driver capacitors fail at 70°C+
3. WAPDA reconnection spike 280V → kills unprotected LED driver
4. Cotton lint / cement dust → 8–15°C extra temperature rise

Fix: metal body IP54 lamp + wide-range 85–265VAC input + SPD on control bus
