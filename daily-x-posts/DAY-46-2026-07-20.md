## Thread 2 — Thermostat Setpoint

❌ European manuals say: set thermostat ON at 40°C
✅ Pakistan rule: set thermostat ON at 35°C

Why? Pakistan July ambient = 47–50°C.
Panel interior hits 65°C before a 40°C thermostat even starts the fan.

Pakistan panel thermostat prices:
• Bimetal (NC contact): Rs. 800–1,500
• Electronic DIN rail: Rs. 1,200–2,200
• Digital with display + 2 relay outputs: Rs. 1,800–3,500

Set ON: 35°C | OFF: 30°C | Alarm: 55°C

## Thread 3 — Filter Mat Replacement

The single biggest cause of panel fan failure in Pakistan?

Blocked filter mat.

Pakistan replacement intervals:
• Cement plant: every 1–2 WEEKS
• Textile/cotton mill: every 2–4 WEEKS
• Flour/rice mill: every 2–3 weeks
• Light industrial: every 2–3 months

At 80% blocked → airflow drops to <20% of rated.
Fan motor overheats → fails.

Stock 3–6 months of filter mats at all times.
At Rs. 400–1,200 per mat, it's the cheapest insurance in your panel.
