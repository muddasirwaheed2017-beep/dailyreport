**Post 3 (Technical value)**

DOL vs Star-Delta starter — which does your tubewell need?

DOL: Full voltage at startup. Fine up to 15 HP. Cheaper. ✅
Star-Delta: Reduces inrush to 33%. Essential above 15 HP. Prevents LESCO feeder trips. 🔌

Star-delta panel costs 50–80% more — but saves you from utility complaints and supply trips.
