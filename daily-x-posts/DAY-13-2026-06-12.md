## Post B — Technical hook (280 chars)

Biggest LFP mistake Pakistani solar installers make:

Not protecting the battery with a DCDB + DC RCCB.

A single ground fault without a DC circuit breaker = battery fires.
IEC 62619 §6.3 requires cell-level protection for system-connected LFP.
