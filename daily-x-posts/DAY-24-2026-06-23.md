### Post 4 — Earthing Reality Check

"I have an RCCB so I'm safe."

Not if:
❌ Your home has no earth conductor (most pre-2005 builds in Pakistan)
❌ Your RCCB is 300mA (fire protection, NOT shock protection)
❌ It's been 2+ years since you pressed the TEST button
❌ You used a Type AC on your solar inverter

Fix all four. 30mA RCCB + earth electrode + monthly test.
