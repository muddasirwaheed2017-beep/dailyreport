## Post B (thread reply 1 — technical hook)

Most Pakistani contractors use the wrong lug for solar DC cables.

A standard tinned copper lug on 6 mm² DC cable at 60°C ambient + 8A continuous load = 15% resistive heating above rated → insulation degradation in 3–5 years.

Use bi-metal (Cu-Al) lugs for aluminum cable, DIN 46235 compression lugs for solar strings.
