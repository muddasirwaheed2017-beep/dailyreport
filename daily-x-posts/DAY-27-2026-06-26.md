## Thread Post 3

⚡ Most common VFD mistake in Pakistan:

Running single-phase supply into a VFD rated 5 HP → motor runs hot → drive trips

Fix: If input is single-phase, buy a VFD rated 150% of your motor.
5 HP motor → buy 7.5 HP VFD.

DC bus derating is real — always size up.
