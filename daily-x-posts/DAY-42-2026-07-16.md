## Tweet 5 (Standalone — common mistake)

Most common Pakistan relay panel mistake:

No freewheeling diode on 24VDC relay coils.

When PLC de-energises the relay, collapsing magnetic field generates 100–200V spike back into PLC output transistor.

First failure: intermittent output.
Second failure: Rs. 80,000 output card.

A Rs. 5 diode across A1-A2 terminals. Every time.
