Tweet 2:
Soft starter vs star-delta: which is better for Pakistani pumps?

Star-delta: two current spikes (star start + delta transition). No protection. Fixed torque.
Soft starter: zero transition spike. Programmable current limit. Built-in phase loss + overload protection.

Cost difference for 30HP: PKR ~45,000 (star-delta panel) vs PKR ~90,000 (soft starter). The soft starter pays back in reduced pump maintenance within 18 months.

Tweet 3:
Pakistan ambient de-rating rule most engineers ignore:

Soft starters are rated at 40°C. Your motor room in Lahore in June hits 50°C.

At 50°C, a 45A rated soft starter is only good for 36A usable.

Your 30HP motor draws 45A FLC.

Fix: always buy one current class up. For 30HP in Pakistan summer → buy a 37kW/55A rated unit, not a 22kW/45A unit.
