## Post 3 — Earthing Reality Check

**[260 chars]**

The most common SPD installation mistake in Pakistan:

No earth connection. Or earth resistance of 100+ Ω.

An SPD without proper earth is 100% useless. The surge has nowhere to go.

Check your earth rod resistance. Target ≤ 10 Ω.

## Post 4 — Uc Parameter Warning

**[255 chars]**

Buying an SPD rated 230V Uc for Pakistan?

Wrong. WAPDA regularly pushes 240–250V.

SPD with Uc = 230V will trigger on normal supply, age prematurely, then fail when you actually need it.

Minimum Uc for Pakistan single-phase: 275V.
