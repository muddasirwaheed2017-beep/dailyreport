IEC 60730 — the standard Pakistani buyers should ask for

  • IEC 60730-1 — general requirements for automatic electrical controls (including thermostats)
  • IEC 60730-2-9 — particular requirements for temperature-sensing controls
  • Pakistani electrical inspection accepts IEC 60730 for residential + light commercial; NEPRA 2026 net-metered solar geyser requires Class B or higher
  • K-Electric + PEPCO solar inspection custom now flags non-certified thermostats on net-metered water heating circuits

NEPRA 2026 solar geyser thermostat rule

Under NEPRA's 2026 prosumer regulations (effective FY2026), any water heater fed by a net-metered solar / hybrid inverter system must have a temperature controller meeting IEC 60730 Class B. This prevents the inverter cycling on inrush from a mechanical thermostat snap-action and protects net-meter measurement accuracy. The rule applies to systems above 12 kW connected load.
