✅ Put a WiFi MCB on circuits where you actually need remote on/off:
  → Geyser (turn off from office at 6 PM, on at 6 AM next day)

❌ Skip WiFi MCB on:
  → Lighting circuits (use WiFi wall switches instead, cheaper)
  → Socket circuits with mixed loads (smart sockets are simpler)

→ 6 kA breaking, real overcurrent protection
→ Real-time V, A, kWh, PF monitoring
→ Tuya / eWeLink / Home Assistant compatible
→ 5-year warranty (vs 1-year on Tuya generic)

Cost premium vs regular MCB: +PKR 7,300 per circuit. Worth it where the remote-control use case justifies. Skip where it doesn't.
