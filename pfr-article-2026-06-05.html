A phase failure relay (also called phase failure protection or single-phasing preventer) is what saves 3-phase motors from being destroyed when one of the three grid phases drops out. In Pakistan this happens routinely — transformer fault, downed line, blown DISCO fuse — and motors that keep running on the remaining 2 phases overheat within seconds. A PKR 1,400 phase failure relay prevents PKR 35,000-180,000 worth of motor rewind.

This guide covers phase failure relay prices in Pakistan for 2026, sizing for tubewells / factory motors / lift motors, wiring with contactors, and the CNC range.

1. What is a phase failure relay?

A phase failure relay continuously monitors all 3 line voltages (R, Y, B). When any one drops below threshold, the relay's output contact opens, breaking the contactor coil circuit and tripping the motor offline within 100-200 milliseconds. The motor stays off until all 3 phases return to normal voltage.

Most relays also detect:

  • Phase reversal — if phase rotation is wrong, motor would spin backwards; relay prevents starting
  • Phase asymmetry / unbalance — if phases are unequal in voltage by more than 10%, relay trips
  • Over/under voltage — outside the 195V-265V envelope

4. Wiring with contactor + overload

  1. Wire 3-phase supply L1, L2, L3 to PFR sensing terminals R, Y, B.
  2. Wire PFR's NC contact in series with: STOP push-button + thermal overload NC + START push-button (latched via contactor NO aux contact) + contactor coil A1, A2.
  3. Tune PFR thresholds: typical under-voltage trip at 195V, over-voltage trip at 265V.
  4. Test: simulate phase loss by switching off one MCB; PFR LED should change colour and motor should trip within 200ms.

5. FAQ

Cheapest phase failure relay in Pakistan 2026?

CNC YCP-3F at PKR 1,400 — phase failure + phase reversal protection. Suitable for tubewells and small factory motors.

Phase failure relay vs phase sequence relay — same thing?

Phase failure detects loss of a phase. Phase sequence detects wrong rotation direction. Most modern PFRs (including CNC YCP-3F) do both.

Will it protect against single phasing on single-phase motors?

No — single-phase motors have only one phase. This relay is for 3-phase motors only. Single-phase motors get overload + voltage protectors instead.

How often does phase failure happen in Pakistan?

Common — 5-20 events per year on a typical DISCO feeder. Cheap insurance via the PKR 1,400 relay.
