Why IP rating matters more than amperage

The most common Pakistani industrial 3-pin socket mistake is buying IP44 for outdoor or rooftop applications. IP44 protects against splash but not jet water or rooftop monsoon. For outdoor / EV / construction sites, always IP66 minimum. The PKR 400-800 premium for IP66 over IP44 is recovered in the first replacement cycle.

NEPRA 2026 EV charging rule

NEPRA's 2026 EV charging infrastructure regulations require IEC 60309 IP44 minimum on Type 1 / Type 2 connector socket installations. Type AC RCCB + Type 2 SPD upstream are mandatory. The 13A BS 1363 socket commonly used for "trickle charging" of EVs is non-compliant for any public or commercial charge point.
